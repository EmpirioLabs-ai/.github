## What Empirio Labs does

Empirio Labs is a specialized AI inference and integration provider. We focus on a small, high-impact set of models and make them genuinely usable in production — not an "everything" aggregator and not a raw GPU host.

- **🧠 Open-source model hosting** — We deploy select open-source models on our own GPU infrastructure with extended context, higher resolution support, and tuned performance.
- **⚡ Optimized proprietary endpoints** — We integrate commercial APIs and partner endpoints, apply our own formatting and behavior layer, and expose them as ready-to-use chat/API endpoints.
- **🚀 Deployment & consulting for your models** — We work with companies and model builders to package, deploy, and operate their models for real users — including distribution.

## Why teams pick Empirio

- **Competitive pricing** — Models on our infra typically run **15–20% cheaper** than other inference providers; select proprietary endpoints up to 20% under standard rates. Fixed per-message pricing on select models — no per-token math required.
- **Per-use, not locked plans** — Pay-as-you-go through our endpoints, even for upstream providers that only sell monthly subscriptions.
- **Higher rate limits than going direct** — Skip restrictive limits and build without hitting walls every few requests.
- **Day-0 support** — New models and capabilities roll out quickly on our stack with routing, pricing, and usage limits wired up from day one.
- **Standard, specialty & tuned models** — Popular models everyone uses, plus open-source and proprietary endpoints you won't find elsewhere — with the full model settings other providers lock away.

## Build with our platform

The EmpirioLabs AI platform helps teams ship production-grade AI experiences with structured agents, managed runs, and realtime status events.

- **Create agents** — configure reusable agents with instructions, models, and tool access
- **Run workflows** — execute prompts and multi-step tasks with consistent inputs and outputs
- **Bring your own knowledge** — upload source material and attach it to the experiences you ship
- **Monitor in realtime** — subscribe to live run events for progress, completion, and failures

### Get started in three steps

1. **Get an API key** — create a project-scoped key in your EmpirioLabs workspace.
2. **Read the quickstart** — follow the [Getting Started](https://docs.empiriolabs.ai/getting-started) guide to make your first authenticated request.
3. **Explore the reference** — use the [API Reference](https://docs.empiriolabs.ai/api-reference) to inspect schemas, examples, and responses.

> Direct sign-ups for the Empirio Labs API are coming soon — keep an eye on the [website](https://empiriolabs.ai) and [changelog](https://empiriolabs-ai.webflow.io/changelog).
